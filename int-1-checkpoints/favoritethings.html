Pizza

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin. It typically has dough, sauce, and cheese. Traditional Italian pizza consists of a thin crust topped with a thin layer of tomato sauce, slices of mozzerella, basil, and olive oil, and baked in an oven. Or at least I think so, I'm not Italian so I don't know.

When people typically think of pizza today they think of the American variant. American pizzas typically have thicker crusts, more sauce and cheese, and more nontraditional toppings (ie bacon, pineapple, peas and mayonnaise, other pizzas).


Pizza is usually consumed as lunch or dinner. It is sold fresh or frozen, in slices or as a whole. Pizza is a common fast food in the Western world. One fast food pizza joint in America I am fond of is Dominos. Here is their website.
